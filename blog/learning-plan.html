Learning Plan

1. What is your long term goal or career pathway?

Ideally, I would like to get into front-end development, focusing on React. In the long term, I want to become a fullstack developer.


2. A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

My strengths would be:

  1. My eagerness to always learn something new
  2. I am a team-player
  3. Strong attention to detail

My weaknesses would be:

  1. I tend to spend unnecessary amount of time on unimportant things
  2. I fail to keep myself motivated to study for longer periods of time

3. What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would like to improve my time management skills.


4. A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme. This should include:

  • An explanation of how you will manage yourself to work productively and safely with other learners, Facilitators and industry/community representatives
  • What you will do when things are building up and starting to get to you

I would treat everyone with respect and empathy, listening to everybody's feedback. I would make sure there are no blindsides and would lend a helping hand whenever I can.

When things are building up and starting to get to me, I wouldn't hesitate to ask for my colleague's feedback if they have suggestions on improving my workflow.

5. A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

I would list out the tasks based on the order of priority and tackle them one by one in a timely fashion.


6. A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team

I expect the facilitation team to guide me when I'm stuck and also to challenge me if I'm breezing through the tasks.
